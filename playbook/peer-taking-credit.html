A peer is taking credit for your work

A colleague keeps presenting your work as theirs — staying silent is costing you visibility.

Say this

"I wanted to flag something. In [specific meeting/email], [my contribution wasn't mentioned]. I'd like us to be deliberate about attributing work accurately going forward."

Steps

  • Address it privately, close in time to a specific incident
  • Describe the pattern with a specific example, not a sweeping accusation
  • Make a specific, forward-looking ask about attribution

Don't

  • Call them out in front of others
  • Complain to mutual colleagues before addressing it directly
  • Wait so long you arrive already frustrated
