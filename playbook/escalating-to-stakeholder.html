Escalating a problem to a senior stakeholder

Something has gone wrong and you need a senior stakeholder to act — they have limited context and limited time.

Say this

"[Project X] is at risk of missing the [date] deadline due to [specific blocker]. I need a decision on [specific ask] by [date]. I'd recommend Option A — [one sentence why]."

"[Issue] is blocked on [decision/action]. Can you [specific ask] by [date]?"

Steps

  • Lead with the situation in one sentence
  • State your ask explicitly — what you need and by when
  • Present options with your recommendation clearly marked

Don't

  • Start with background before stating the problem
  • Be vague about what you need
  • Escalate without a proposed path forward
