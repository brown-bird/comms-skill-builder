A colleague missed a promotion — and you got it

Your colleague just found out they didn't get promoted — and you did.

Say this

"I heard the news and I didn't want to just pretend it didn't happen. I imagine that's really disappointing — you've put a lot into this. I just wanted to say I see it."

Steps

  • Find a private, low-pressure moment to acknowledge it briefly
  • Lead with recognition of their experience, not your news
  • Give them space after — let them set the pace

Don't

  • Celebrate visibly near them
  • Avoid them entirely
  • Over-apologise or minimise your achievement
