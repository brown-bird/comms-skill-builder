Saying no to working on the weekend

Your manager asks you to work the weekend on something that isn't a genuine emergency.

Say this

"I'm not able to work this weekend. I can have this done by [Monday/Tuesday morning]. If there's a specific piece that's genuinely time-sensitive, let me know."

"Before I figure out logistics — is there a hard deadline driving the weekend timeline, or is Monday morning workable?"

Steps

  • Confirm whether the urgency is real before responding
  • Name what you can and can't do, and offer an alternative timeline
  • Keep your tone neutral and practical — this is scheduling, not confrontation

Don't

  • Say yes when you mean no
  • Over-explain or over-apologise
  • Give a flat no with no alternative
