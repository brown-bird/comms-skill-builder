Negotiating a flexible working arrangement

You want to negotiate a change to your working arrangement and your manager has concerns about team consistency.

Say this

"I'd like to propose working from home on Mondays and Fridays. My output hasn't changed on remote days. I'd commit to being in Tuesday through Thursday. Would you be open to trying it for a month?"

Steps

  • Make a specific, concrete proposal with days and conditions
  • Frame it around shared interests — productivity and mutual trust
  • Propose a trial period to lower the stakes of a yes

Don't

  • Frame it as a demand
  • Rely purely on personal preference
  • Make a vague ask like "more flexibility"
