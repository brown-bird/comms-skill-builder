Principle 07

Confirm before you continue

Consent-based Sequencing

Instead of presenting your full argument when proposing something, pause at each step and check that they're with you.

Playbook entries

  • Pitching an idea when the timing feels off
  • Getting a skeptical peer to support a process change
  • Proposing a significant change
  • Giving critical feedback to a peer
