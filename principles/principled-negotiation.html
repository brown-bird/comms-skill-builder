Principle 05

Negotiate on interests, not positions

Principled Negotiation

Instead of debating positions when you're at an impasse, ask what's important to them and name what's important to you.

Playbook entries

  • Negotiating a flexible working arrangement
  • Saying no to working on the weekend
  • Getting a skeptical peer to support a process change
