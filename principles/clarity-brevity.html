Principle 02

Say only what needs to be said

Clarity & Brevity

Instead of burying your ask in context when you need something, lead with the point and name the specific action.

Playbook entries

  • Escalating a problem to a senior stakeholder
  • A peer is taking credit for your work
  • Negotiating a flexible working arrangement
  • Saying no to working on the weekend
  • Giving critical feedback to a peer
Built as a living reference — updated as situations teach new lessons.
