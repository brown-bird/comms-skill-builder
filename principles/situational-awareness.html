Principle 04

Adapt to the situation

Situational Awareness

Instead of using your default approach when entering a conversation, read the room and calibrate to what's happening.

Playbook entries

  • Pitching an idea when the timing feels off
  • A colleague missed a promotion — and you got it
  • Escalating a problem to a senior stakeholder
  • A peer is taking credit for your work
  • Proposing a significant change
Built as a living reference — updated as situations teach new lessons.
