# Communications Skill Builder

A static website that serves as a **communications situational playbook** — a practical reference for navigating real-life interpersonal scenarios with clarity, empathy, and intention.

---

## Project Overview

Communications Skill Builder is a personal development tool designed to help individuals communicate more effectively in everyday situations. Rather than abstract theory, it offers **situational guidance** — concrete frameworks and response strategies organized around the type of interaction you're facing.

The playbook is grounded in six core principles:

1. **Empathy & Tone** — Adjusting your approach based on the emotional context of the situation
2. **Clarity & Brevity** — Saying exactly what needs to be said, without ambiguity or excess
3. **Conflict Resolution** — De-escalating tension and moving toward shared understanding
4. **Situational Awareness** — Reading the room and adapting to the dynamics at play
5. **Principled Negotiation** — Focusing on interests rather than positions to reach durable agreements
6. **Influencing Without Authority** — Moving people forward through trust, framing, and genuine connection

## Playbook Format

Each situational entry follows a consistent structure:

- **Situation** — What's happening and why it's tricky
- **Applicable Principles** — Which of the six principles are most relevant
- **What to Avoid** — Common missteps and why they backfire
- **Recommended Approach** — Step-by-step guidance
- **Example Language** — Sample phrases or scripts to adapt
- **Follow-Up** — How to close the loop or sustain the outcome

---

## Design Philosophy

This is a **static site** — no backend, no database, no login. It's fast, portable, and can be hosted anywhere (GitHub Pages, Netlify, etc.). The focus is entirely on the content and usability of the playbook itself.

The interface should feel like a trusted reference guide: calm, uncluttered, and easy to navigate under pressure.
